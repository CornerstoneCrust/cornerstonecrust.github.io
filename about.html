Rooted in Family, Fired by Passion About Us

Cornerstone Crust was founded by two cousins from Birmingham with one shared goal: to bring people together through food that’s made with integrity. What started as a passion project quickly became a mission — to serve handcrafted, wood-fired pizza that blends tradition with bold, creative flair.

We grew up surrounded by family meals, good music, and stories told around a hot plate — and that same warmth is what we bring to every event. Whether we’re at a local market, festival, or your wedding, we fire every pizza with purpose, using the best ingredients and never cutting corners. The cornerstone of our business? Faith, flavour, and community.

We don’t just serve pizza. We serve people.
